    Rules of Basketball

    1. Traveling

      -Traveling occurs when a player holding the ball moves one or both of their feet illegally. This usually involves taking too many steps without dribbling the ball.

    2. Double Dribble

      -Double dribble is when a player dribbles the ball, stops, and then begins to dribble again without passing or shooting the ball. It is considered a violation.

    3. Three-Second Violation

      -The three-second violation is when an offensive player remains in the key or paint area (the area under the basket) for more than three seconds at a time without actively moving away.
